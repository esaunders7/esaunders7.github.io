About Me

My name is Emma Saunders, and I am 21 years old. I attend the University of South Carolina in Columbia, but I was born in Savannah, Georgia. I am one of three siblings and the oldest. When I was younger, I enjoyed playing every sport including soccer, basketball, cheerleading, swimming, and running. However it was not until the end of middle school that I got introduced to the sport of golf. This game taught me patience, time-management, and intelligence which I have taken with me to this university.

My favorite activities to do in my spare time include playing golf with my family, spending time with my friends, boating, traveling, and reading. I also love sports and watch games or matches whenever I can. One of my other favorite hobies includes listening to music. I like a large variety and always try to find a cheap, live band to see or travel to concerts. My favorite one so far has been either Whiskey Myers or Post Malone. I hope to travel and see more bands/artists in the near future.

My Future Career Plans

After I finish my undergraduate degree I plan on attending graduate school. I am hoping that this includes medical or physician assistant school. After this, I will enter into a career at a hospital near either Savannah, Georgia or Charleston, South Carolina. My dream would be to work as either a heart surgeon's assistant or as an endodontist in a smaller office. These jobs are very different but each contain different aspects of what I want. One is a high pressure, exciting job while the other has a nice work, life balance. I am hoping graduate school helps me narrow down my interests.

I have always known that I wanted to work in the medical field. I have a passion for helping people, especially kids, so when I was younger I thought I wanted to go into neonatology or pediatric surgery. I have spent the last two years shadowing and interviewing people in my potential career fields and know that these jobs are more demanding than I want my life to be. Besides working, I want to have a big family and need a job that has a manageable work/life balance.
